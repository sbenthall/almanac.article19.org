Technical Committee 224 - Working Group 18 Personal Identification | CEN/TC 224

This group develops standards to strengthen the interoperability and security of personal identification systems and personal devices. The increasingly widespread use of biometric technologies for personal identification and authentication poses major challenges to users' privacy, specifically the covert collection of data and in the form of function creep, whereby these technologies are gradually used beyond the purpose of their original intention.
