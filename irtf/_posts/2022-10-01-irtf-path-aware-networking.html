Path Aware Networking Research Group | panrg

This research group aims to research the development of path awareness within the transport and application layers, whereby endpoints can discover the properties of various data paths, and select data paths for the exchange of data and information. Currently, endpoints in IP-based networks have little information about the data paths in which their traffic is carried, nor have control about the data path beyond reaching their destination address. In the best case, endpoints can potentially select data paths that are more trusted and less vulnerable to snooping and monitoring from malicious actors, protecting the privacy and security of data.
