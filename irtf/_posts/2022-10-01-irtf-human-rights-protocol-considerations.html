Human Rights Protocol Considerations | hrpc

This research group explores the relationship between internet protocols and human rights. It proposes guidelines to inform future protocol development and aims to increase awareness on the importance of the technical workings of the internet for human rights. It also aims to prepare participants in HRPC to raise human rights concerns directly with specific working groups.

  • Current drafts: Guidelines for Human Rights Protocol and Architecture Considerations aims to be an informational guideline for human rights considerations for developers working on network protocols and architectures. Internet Protocols and the Human Rights to Freedom of Association and Assembly aims to be an informational document that outlines the relationship between internet architecture and the right to peaceful assembly and association online.
