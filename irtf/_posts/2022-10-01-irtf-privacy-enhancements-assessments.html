Privacy Enhancements and Assessments Research Group | pearg

This research group aims to convene participants to discuss and review the use and applicability of new privacy enhancing technologies and mechanisms. It also documents existing research on new and existing privacy assessment methodologies to inform future development of protocol specifications.

  • Current drafts: A Survey of Worldwide Censorship Techniques aims to be an informational document that describes the technical mechanisms state actors employ to block and censor internet traffic.
  • Working group: Privacy Enhancements and Assessments Research Group | pearg
