The European Telecommunications Standards Institute | ETSI

ETSI is a standards developing organisation responsible for developing standards related to telecommunications, broadcasting and other electronic communications networks and services. It only accepts organisations as members (individual contributors must be affiliated with and representing a member organisation). Set up in response to proposals from the European Commission, ETSI is officially recognised as a European Standards Organisation to develop ICT standards and support for European regulations and legislations through the development of Harmonised European Standards. If a manufacturer wants to enter its product into the EU Single Market, it must comply with certain minimal safety and procedural requirements, known as a harmonised standard.
