Alliance for Telecommunications Industry Solutions | ATIS

ATIS is the North American regional partner organisation for the 3GPP. It develops technical and operational standards for the ICT industry. It has 200 member companies that are currently working on various ICT networks and applications, including the All-IP transition in core and access networks, 5G, virtualisation of network functions, big data analytics, cloud services, device solutions, cybersecurity, and network evolution. To participate in ATIS, an organisation must be a stakeholder from the ICT sector and apply to be a member. Civil society organisations can apply to become affiliate members.
