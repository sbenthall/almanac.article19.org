3rd Generation Partnership Project | 3GPP

3GPP is a membership-based organisation that unites various national and regional standard development organisations for mobile telecommunications. Participants that are representatives of big telecommunication companies such as Verizon, Vodafone, China Mobile, and América Móvil can become individual members through their national and regional SDOs. Their scope of work includes cellular telecommunications technologies including radio access, core network and services; as well as non-radio access to the core network and interworking with non-3GPP networks. 3GPP standards are contribution-driven by organisational and individual partners in working groups and at the technical specification group level.
