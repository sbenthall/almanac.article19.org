The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) develops a large range of international standards for modern telecommunications hardware, such as standards for networks that allow devices to connect to the internet, including Ethernet, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi. IEEE also works on standards for robotics, smart cities, and artificial intelligence.
