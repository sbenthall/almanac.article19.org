Service and Aspects Working Group 3 - Lawful Intercept | SA3-LI

Within SA3, the sub-working group SA3-LI provides the requirements and specifications for lawful interception in 3GPP systems. Lawful intercept is a regulatory and legal obligation towards law enforcement agencies that communication service providers must comply with. This obligation allows the appropriate authorities to perform interception of communication traffic for specific users. In recent years, other than private sector companies such as Nokia and Ericsson, the main actors involved in influencing and shaping these standards are governments primarily from Europe, North America, and their respective national law enforcement agencies. The other actors involved are telecoms infrastructure providers, network providers, cybersecurity companies, network equipment providers and consulting groups working on behalf of governments.
