Digital Identity Working Group

This group aims to standardise the requirements for blockchain-based digital identity systems. Digital identity systems can pose issues of data security and identity fraud, and could prevent marginalised communities from accessing their online identity. The group also aims to enable acceptance and interoperability between different digital identity systems through the use of digital identity blockchains.

  • Published standard: P3210 Blockchain-based Digital Identity System Framework establishes requirements for blockchain-based digital identity systems. It provides a definition for digital identity and addresses the creation and authentication of distributed digital identity (DID), a form of digital identity management in which control over information is decentralised.
  • Working group: Digital Identity Working Group
