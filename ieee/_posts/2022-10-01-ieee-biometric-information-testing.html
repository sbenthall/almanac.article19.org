Biometric Information Testing Working Group | BITWG

This group aims to build a secure, low cost, and industry-wide accepted fingerprint test system for mobile devices. Testing fingerprint verification is critical given its use for identification, authentification, and recognition purposes. The design and evaluation, including application and hardware specifications of these biometric test systems, can impact how personally identifiable information is stored and shared, impacting peopleâ€™s privacy.
