Biometrics Open Protocol Working Group | BOP

This group aims to build frameworks to store biometric data securely against cyber attacks. The market’s uptake in biometric technologies combined with the increase in cyber attacks can lead to the identifying and tracking individuals using their biometric data. BOPs aim to handle biometric data securely by breaking up information – referred to as distributed data – and establishing communication with the server through end-to-end authentication.

  • Published standard: IEEE Standard for Biometric Privacy provides a framework for a software-based system of identity assertion that alerts users when and where transactions and log-ins occur, ensuring that only authorised users perform functions.
