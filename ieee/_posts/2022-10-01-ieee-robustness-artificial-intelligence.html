Robustness of Artificial Intelligence-based Service Working Group | RAIS_WG

RAIS_WG aims to define specifications to guide developers in building AI-based systems, including image recognition systems. These specifications are set out to test for accuracy, ensure resistance against malicious attacks by third parties, and ensure that the system cannot be easily fooled by adversarial attacks that expose its weaknesses.
