How to use the dashboard?

Isolating an actor Isolate an actor in the legend on the right by double clicking on them. Toggle actors by clicking on them.

Exporting plots Plots can be exported by clicking on the camera icon. The export will always show the current selection and current zoom level. The exported file is a scalable vector image with a transparent background.
