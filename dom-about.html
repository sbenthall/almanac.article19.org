Authorship

The authorship graph shows participation of actors and their affiliation in writing RFC drafts in a working group or an SDO.

Influence

The influence graph shows the amount of emails sent to working group related mailing lists over time. Or: who generated most noise?

Leadership

The leadership graph shows the chairs of a working group and their affiliation over time.

How to use the dashboard?

Isolating an actor Isolate an actor in the legend on the right by double clicking on them. Toggle actors by clicking on them.

Exporting plots Plots can be exported by clicking on the camera icon. The export will always show the current selection and current zoom level. The exported file is a scalable vector image with a transparent background.
