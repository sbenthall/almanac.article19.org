Study Group 13 Future Networks | ITU‑T SG13

This study group develops standards for cloud computing, 5G mobile communications, and quantum key distribution networks, as well as addressing the requirements, architectures, functional capabilities and application programming for future networks. The SG has also been setting standards for network aspects of the Internet of Things (IoT) and the application of AI and machine learning for communication networks such as 5G. As some of these technologies become more ubiquitous, the collection of sensitive and personally identifiable data, as well as the security of that data, becomes more important.

  • Current work items: Focus Group Technologies for Network 2030 (FG NET-2030) is tasked to study the architecture, requirements, use cases and capabilities of the currently emerging and future network technologies (“New IP”). This may negatively impact the interoperability, security, and governance of the existing principles of IP-based networks.
