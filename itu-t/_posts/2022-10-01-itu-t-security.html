Study Group 17 Security | ITU‑T SG17

This study group develops standards that address security aspects of telecommunication/ICT and applications, including the Internet of Things (IoT), smartphones, web services, 5G, and technologies being studied and standardised in other study groups across ITU-T. As individuals interact with ICT applications in their everyday lives, an increasingly large amount of personal information and data that can be used to identify and track people is collected.

  • Current work items: The Guideline on Blockchain as a Service (BaaS) Security includes sections that pose potential security vulnerabilities as data cannot be erased in blockchain technologies. Personally identifiable information such as biometric information cannot be collected or stored on a blockchain as this has serious impacts on the security of the technology and individuals privacy.
  • Working group: Study Group 17 Security | ITU‑T SG17
