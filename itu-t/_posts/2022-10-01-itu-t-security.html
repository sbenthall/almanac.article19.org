Study Group 17 on Security | ITU-T SG17

SG17 develops standards that address security aspects of telecommunications, ICTs, and related applications being studied and standardised in other study groups across ITU-T. Security principles, including confidentiality, integrity, and availability, are important determinants in how well technologies and systems protect or threaten people’s privacy.

  • Standard under development The Guideline on Blockchain as a Service (BaaS) addresses potential security vulnerabilities in the use of particular blockchain-based technologies. The storage of personally identifiable information, including biometric data, on a blockchain has serious implications for people’s privacy and security.
