Private Advertising Technology Community Group | PAT CG

This group aims to develop web features and APIs that support advertising while also safeguarding user privacy. Advertisers and publishers can measure how effective their ads are by determining whether a user clicks on an ad and ends up purchasing a product shown through the advertiser’s cookie. This can allow advertisers to track users across the internet based on websites where their ads have been placed.

  • Current proposals: Interoperable Private Attribution (IPA) aims to stop linking users through cookies by developing linkages based on an anonymous identifier. This could prevent users’ browsing behaviour from being tracked while measuring the effectiveness of online ads.
  • Working group: Private Advertising Technology Community Group | PAT CG
