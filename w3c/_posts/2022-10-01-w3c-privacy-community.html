Privacy Community Group | Privacy CG

The Privacy Community Group aims to develop web features and APIs that improve user privacy through browser enhancements. These features primarily occur in the form of how cookies and browsing information is stored and shared. Security vulnerabilities can allow malicious actors to obtain a user's session cookies to impersonate the user, perform actions on behalf of the user and gain access to sensitive information.

  • Current drafts: Cookies Having Independent Partitioned State is aimed at preventing third-party cookies from linking the users across different sites while functioning within one specific site.
