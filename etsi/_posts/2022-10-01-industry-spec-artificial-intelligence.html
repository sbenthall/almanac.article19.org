Industry Specification Group on Securing Artificial Intelligence | ISG SAI

The group is responsible for developing technical standards to mitigate threats arising from the deployment of AI throughout multiple ICT-related industries. It focuses on using AI to enhance security, mitigating against attacks that leverage AI, and securing AI itself from attack. This is important because autonomous mechanical and computing entities may make decisions that act against the relying parties either by design or as a result of malicious intent.

  • Published standard: GR SAI 006 The group report provides an overview on the role of hardware in the security of AI systems. It addresses the vulnerabilities and mitigations available in hardware to prevent attacks and how AI systems can be used for the protection of hardware.
