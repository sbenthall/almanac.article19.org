Technical Committee for Intelligent Transport Systems | TC ITS

TC ITS develops standards to support the development and implementation of intelligent transport systems (ITS), including service provision across networks for transport and vehicles. Standards address the overall communication architecture as well as management, access, and security elements, with the aim of increasing travel safety, minimising environmental impact, improving traffic management, and maximising the benefits of transportation.
