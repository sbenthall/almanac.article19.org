Technical Committee for Intelligent Transport Systems | TC ITS

The technical committee develops standards to support the development and implementation of intelligent transport systems (ITS) service provision across networks for transport and vehicles. Standards address the overall communication architecture, management, access and security with the aim of increasing travel safety, minimising environmental impact, improving traffic management and maximising the benefits of transportation.
