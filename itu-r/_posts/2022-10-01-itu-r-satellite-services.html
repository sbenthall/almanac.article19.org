Study Group 4 Satellite Services | ITU-R SG 4

The study group develops standards for satellite service systems and networks for various uses, including mobile and broadcasting satellites. The group also sets out spectrum allocations for satellite services and in turn, determines which operators using satellite services can provide telecommunication services for users. This ultimately affects how people access information and communicate with each other.

  • Current work items: Reviewing uplink considerations in a spectrum band for broadcasting satellite services in the Americas. Uplink is the data flow between ground stations and a satellite. It affects the quality of service of satellite communications which can be used to provide affordable connectivity to users living in rural communities.
