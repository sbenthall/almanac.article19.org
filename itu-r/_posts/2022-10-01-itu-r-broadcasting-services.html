Study Group 6 Broadcasting Service | ITU‑R SG 6

This study group develops standards for various stages of the broadcasting chain, from the production of programs to how they are delivered to the audience. This includes discussions around spectrum allocation and quality of service for broadcasting. Spectrum allocations set in this group determine which services have access in order to broadcast information. This ultimately impacts media plurality among broadcasting services and the choice people have when consuming media and information.

  • Current work items: Reviewing the spectrum use and needs for broadcasting services from Europe, Africa, the former Soviet Union, and the Middle East west of the Persian Gulf.
  • Working group: Study Group 6 Broadcasting Service | ITU‑R SG 6
