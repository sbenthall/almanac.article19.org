Artificial Intelligence | JTC 1/SC 42

This subcommittee develops standards for artificial intelligence (AI) applications and the broader AI ecosystem. It also provides guidance to ISO and IEC committees developing AI applications. The exponential use of technologies reliant on intensive data collection enables function creep, whereby these technologies are gradually used beyond their original intention.

  • Standards in development: Verifying AI systems, an impact assessment for AI systems, functional safety of AI systems
  • Working group: Artificial Intelligence | JTC 1/SC 42
