Technical Committee on Intelligent Transportation Systems | ISO/TC 204

This committee, convened solely by ISO, produces standards on the overall system aspects and infrastructure aspects of intelligent transport systems (ITS). ITS comprises various forms of information and communication technologies embedded in different modes of transport that interact with each other through, for example, sensor technologies that monitor roads and vehicles.
