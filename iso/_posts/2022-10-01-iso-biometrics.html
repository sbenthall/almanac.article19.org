Joint Technical Committee on Information Technology, Subcommittee on Biometrics | JTC 1/SC 37

Under JTC1, convened jointly by ISO and IEC, this subcommittee develops international standards and technical reports to ensure that biometric recognition systems and applications are interoperable, scalable, and secure. The uptake of biometric technologies, combined with increases in cyberattacks, can expose individuals whose biometric data are implicated in these systems and leave them vulnerable to being identified, tracked, and monitored.

  • Published technical report: A study of the differential impact of demographic factors in biometric recognition system performance published in 2021, addresses bias in biometric systems.
  • Working group: Joint Technical Committee on Information Technology, Subcommittee on Biometrics | JTC 1/SC 37
