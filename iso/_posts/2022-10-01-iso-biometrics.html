Biometrics | JTC 1/SC 37

This subcommittee develops international standards and technical reports to ensure that biometric recognition systems and applications are interoperable, scalable and secure. The use of biometrics in everyday applications means that more personal information is being collected, increasing the risk of identifying and tracking individuals.

  • Published standard: A study of the differential impact of demographic factors in biometric recognition system performance published in 2021, aims to identify bias in biometric systems.
  • Working group: Biometrics | JTC 1/SC 37
