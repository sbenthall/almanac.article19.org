Sustainable Cities and Communities | ISO/TC 268

This committee develops standards on the requirements, frameworks, guidance, and tools to build resilient and sustainable smart cities and communities. Systems designed off Internet of Things (IoT) technologies (devices with technologies that are connected to each other) can capture extensive data and create an environment that normalises constant tracking and monitoring. The committee defines basic concepts as well as specifications for measurement, reporting, and verification of these technologies. The centralisation and integration of data for vital infrastructure and services could lead to increased data breaches or attacks, user privacy issues, and pervasive government surveillance.

  • Standards in development: A unified data framework for the application of digital technologies in smart city infrastructure is being developed to improve the interoperability of services. This involves integrating data from the city management framework, ICT, public facilities, services databases, and ensuring interoperability.
  • Working group: Sustainable Cities and Communities | ISO/TC 268
